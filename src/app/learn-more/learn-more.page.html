About

Nom kitties is a game developed by Alberto Sosa, it is free to play, I made it for a hackathon to explore the wonderful tools in AWS.

It is a game of strategy and skill, where you collect the most treats to win!

Local Games

Local games are played on the same device, and are great for playing with friends in the same room.

Online Games

Online games are played with friends over the internet, and are great for playing with friends who are far away.

To Host games, and play online with friends you have to make an account. I use aws cognito and have no access to your passwords or anything like that.

Conclusion
